Snake Game

Snake game is a monochrome arcade video game. Using four directional buttons, each player moves their snake turning at 90 degree angles. To win, a player must last longer before hitting something. Snake is the common name for a video game concept where the player maneuvers a line which grows in length, with the line itself being a primary obstacle. The snake continually gets longer as it moves. The player loses when the snake runs into the screen border or itself. Each item eaten makes the snake longer, making levels more difficult.

War - Card Game

War is a simple card game, typically played by two players using a standard playing card deck. The objective of the game is to win all of the cards. The deck is divided evenly among the players. In unison, each player reveals the top card of their deck. The player with the higher card wins. Aces are high, and suits are ignored. If the two cards played are of equal value, then there is a "war". This repeats until one player's score is higher than their opponent's.
