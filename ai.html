Wumpus Game

Wumpus is a text-based adventure game set in a series of caves connected by tunnels. In one of the caves is a "Wumpus", which the player is attempting to kill, and three other caves contain bottomless pits. The player starts moving through the connected caves. The caves are in complete darkness, so the player cannot see into adjacent caves; instead, upon moving to a new empty cave, the game describes if they can smell a Wumpus, or feel a breeze from a pit. Entering a cave with a pit ends the game due to the player falling in, or entering the cave with the Wumpus also ends it since Wumpus will kill the player. If the player chooses to fire an arrow and hits the Wumpus, player win. In the game, The A.I randomly selects the caves for the Wumpus and pits so that each time the player enters into the cave, it gets harder.

Kalaha Game

The game board has 6 small pits, called 'houses', on each side; and a big pit, called 'store', at each end. The object of the game is to capture more seeds than A.I. At the beginning of the game, three seeds are placed in each house. The player controls the six houses and their seeds on his side of the board. The player's score is the number of seeds in their store. Players take turns sowing their seeds. On a turn, the player removes all seeds from one of the houses under their control moving counter-clockwise. If the last sown seed lands in an empty house owned by the player, and the opposite house contains seeds, both the last seed and the opposite seeds are captured and placed into the player's store. If the last sown seed lands in the player's store, the player gets an additional move. There is no limit on the number of moves a player can make in their turn. When one player no longer has any seeds in any of their houses, the game ends and the player with the most seeds in their store wins. It is possible for the game to end in a draw.
